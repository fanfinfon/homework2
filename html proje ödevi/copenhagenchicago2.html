Chicago



-In this city where America's first skyscraper is located (The Home Insurance Building), in addition to the skyscraper in the city center overlooking the lake and colorful streets, there are parks and gardens where various species of poultry live in peace and undisturbed.

-Chicago lays claim to numerous regional characteristics that reflect the city's ethnic and working-class roots. These include the nationally renowned deep dish pizza; This style is said to have originated at Pizzeria Uno.

-The city is very popular for its basketball team (Chicago Bulls). It is known for Michael Jordan and many stars played for the Chicago Bulls and the championships earned. The indoor sports hall called "United Center", where this team is hosted, is on the "Near West Side" of Chicago.
