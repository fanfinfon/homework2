The Reichstag Building is the seat of the German Bundestag, located in Berlin. It is an iconic symbol of German democracy.

Mount Mitte is a popular recreational area in Berlin, offering stunning views of the city. It's a great place for outdoor activities and relaxation.

The Berlin Wall was a barrier that divided Berlin from 1961 to 1989. Today, remnants of the wall serve as a historical reminder of the city's past.
